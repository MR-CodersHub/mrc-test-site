At MR CODERS HUB, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website and interact with our training programs and career services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us through our contact, enquiry, and enrollment forms. This may include:

  • Personal Identifiers: Name, email address, and phone number.
  • Course Details: Program selections (e.g., Full Stack Dev, Data Analysis) and learning goals.
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, and device information collected via automated tracking.

2. How We Use Your Information

MR CODERS HUB uses the collected data for the following purposes:

  • To process and manage your course enrollments and enquiry requests.
  • To provide technical support and respond to student inquiries.
  • To analyze website traffic and optimize the learning experience.
  • To send updates regarding your program status or academic announcements (where opted-in).

3. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies to enhance your browsing experience and analyze site performance. Cookies are small data files stored on your device. You can instruct your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent.

4. Data Sharing and Security

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. We may share information with trusted service providers (such as hosting partners or email services) solely to operate our business and serve you. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data, though no method of electronic transmission is 100% secure.

5. Data Retention and Your Rights

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy. You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request the correction or deletion of your data.
  • Withdraw consent for academic communications at any time.
